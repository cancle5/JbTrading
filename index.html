JB Values

JB Values is a roblox jailbreak trading website where you can discover jailbreak values. Not only can you find a jailbreak value list here, but we also have a trade calculator, a trade finder, and much more!

Values

Our values are by our community, which makes them extremely accurate. Members give suggestions in our discord server. The site team will review the suggestion and a poll will be created. Then the community will vote on whether they think it's actually accurate or not.

Calculator

In addition to having values, we also provide a wide range of features to help you in trading. Use our trade calculator to check if your trade is worth it or not!
